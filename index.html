Nice to meet you

My name is Mark Claus Nunes, I am a Brazilian designer and front end developer. I moved to England with the purpose of challenging myself both personally and professionally. I love design and I am always studying, practically applying what I have learned, and sharing ideas through the articles I write for various online publications. Currently working as Web Designer and Front End Developer at Venntro.

Good, clean design creates good, clean results.

Many designers create by instinct, but I believe design is grounded in knowledge, logic and psychology. The result can have beautiful form, but it must also perform its intended function. I approach projects first as information designer, then as visual artist and finally with great regard to the tangible final product - how it looks, communicates and how it isunderstood. Transforming pixels to meaningful experiences. I bring passion, experience & creativity to every project, so I am looking to work with people who feel the same about the service they provide.
